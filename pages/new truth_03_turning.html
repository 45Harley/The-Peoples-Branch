Rung 3: Repentance

Repentance is not guilt. It is not self-flagellation. It is the moment when the soul turns—toward truth, toward healing, toward alignment. No one can force this step. But when it comes, it cracks the crust that hid the wound.

“What needs to be released? Admitted? Faced inwardly?”

Reflection Prompt

Without spectacle or defense, write down what you now see as your part. The omission. The silence. The distortion. The betrayal.

This is where transformation begins—if we let it.
