Rung 4: Reform Imagined

This rung isn’t about retribution—it’s about refusal. Refusal to repeat harm, refusal to rebuild the old house with old plans. Here we design: systems, habits, safeguards, symbols, ceremonies. Reform is imagination with integrity.

“What might restoration look like—not only for the harmed, but for the whole?”

Reflection Prompt

Sketch a possible future: what structures would make the harm less likely? What truths need to be baked into our blueprints, our norms, our tools?

You are not alone in this. Others will build from what you offer.
