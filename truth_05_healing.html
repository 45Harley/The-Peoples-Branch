Rung 5: Healing Begins

Healing doesn’t mean forgetting. It doesn’t mean excusing. It means living forward—with the wound integrated, the truth intact, and the future unshackled. Healing is not a reward. It’s a right. And sometimes, a revolt.

“What does healing mean—if not forgetting?”

Reflection Prompt

What does healing look like *for you*, right now? What rhythms, relationships, or rituals help you hold the memory without being held *by* it?

Name one next kindness you could offer yourself—or someone else still carrying the burn.
