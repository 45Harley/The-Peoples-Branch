Truth 05: Healing

Healing isn’t always a finish line. Sometimes it’s a breath. A pause. A moment of noticing that something hurts less than it used to.

This rung invites you to name what has begun to mend—and what still needs tending. Healing is not linear, but it is real.

Choose a voice to explore the prompt:

Plain: What has begun to heal? What still needs care?
Poetic: What scar sings now instead of stinging?
Analytical: Identify areas of personal or communal restoration, and those still in process.
Playful: What part of you got a bandage and a hug—and what’s still waiting?
