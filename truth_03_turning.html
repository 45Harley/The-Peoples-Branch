Truth 03: Turning

There’s a moment—sometimes quiet, sometimes explosive—when we begin to turn. Away from something. Toward something else. A shift in direction, even if we don’t yet know where it leads.

This rung invites you to name a turning point. A moment when you began to change your mind, your path, or your sense of what matters.

Choose a voice to explore the prompt:

Plain: When did you begin to change direction?
Poetic: What wind caught your sails and turned you toward a new horizon?
Analytical: Identify a pivotal moment that altered your trajectory or worldview.
Playful: What made you say, “Nope! Time to pivot!”?
