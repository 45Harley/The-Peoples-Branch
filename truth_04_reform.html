Truth 04: Reform

After the turning comes the reshaping. The slow, deliberate work of reforming what no longer fits—inside us, around us, between us.

This rung invites you to name something you’ve reformed: a belief, a behavior, a system, a story. What did you change, and why?

Choose a voice to explore the prompt:

Plain: What have you reshaped or reformed in your life or community?
Poetic: What old scaffolding did you tear down to build something truer?
Analytical: Describe a structural or conceptual change you initiated or embraced.
Playful: What did you fix, remix, or totally flip upside down?
