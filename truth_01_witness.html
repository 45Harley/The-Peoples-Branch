Rung 1: Harm Witnessed

This is not a space to defend or deflect. It is a space to name what was true. To stop making excuses—for ourselves or others. To remember the impact, not the intention. To speak with clarity that does not require permission.

“Describe the harm—not abstractly, but in its impact.”

Reflection Prompt

What happened that wounded you—or someone else? Where does the ache live now?

Write it in a way that a future self could understand, and not look away.
