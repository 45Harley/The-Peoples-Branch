Truth 02: Exposure

Some truths arrive like lightning. Sudden. Blinding. Unavoidable. Others creep in slowly, peeling back layers we didn’t know we wore.

This rung invites you to name a moment of exposure—when something hidden became visible, and you couldn’t unsee it.

Choose a voice to explore the prompt:

Plain: What truth came to light—even when it hurt?
Poetic: What veil tore open and left you blinking in the light?
Analytical: Describe a moment when new information disrupted your worldview.
Playful: What truth popped out like a jack-in-the-box and made you go “Whoa!”?
